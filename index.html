How SecretWeave Works

SecretWeave shifts every letter in your message forward or backward by a number you choose (1–25). Non-letter characters like spaces and punctuation pass through unchanged. The same shift number is used to decrypt — keep it secret!

01

Write your message

Type any text. Only letters get shifted — spaces and symbols stay as-is.

02

Pick a shift

Choose any number from 1 to 25. The higher, the more scrambled your message looks.

03

Share securely

Send your cipher text and share the shift number through a separate channel.
